Case Studies

Projects we've

Real work, real results. Here's what we've built for other businesses.

B2B Services

Automated accounts receivable

A B2B services firm was chasing invoices manually — late payments, missed follow-ups, and hours lost to admin. We built an AI agent that triggers on deal close, generates invoices, sends Stripe payment links, monitors status, chases overdue accounts, and updates the CRM automatically.

  • Manual invoicing eliminated
  • Payment cycle reduced by 12 days
  • Zero missed follow-ups since launch
Freight Forwarding

Customs declaration pre-check

A freight forwarder was losing time to rejected customs declarations — wrong commodity codes, missing fields, mismatched values. We built an AI layer that scans incoming shipment documents, extracts key data, cross-references against tariff schedules, and flags errors before submission.

  • Declaration errors reduced by 85%
  • HMRC queries dropped significantly
  • Pre-check runs in under 30 seconds
Manufacturing

Voice-to-report for shift handovers

Shift supervisors at a manufacturing plant were writing handover notes by hand — inconsistent, often incomplete, and hard to act on. We built a voice-to-report system that transcribes spoken notes, structures them into standardised reports, flags safety incidents, and pushes summaries to management.

  • Handover time cut from 25 mins to 5
  • 100% of safety flags now captured
  • Handwritten logs fully replaced
